Events

Our Events

“Our events bring together compassion, care, and community.”
From health camps to food drives, each initiative is a step towards making a positive difference in people’s lives.

Food Drive

"No one should sleep hungry." A small effort from us can bring a big smile to someone's face. Join us in our Food Drive, where we come together to serve humanity by sharing meals with those in need. Because kindness served on a plate can fill not just stomachs, but hearts too.

Free Medical Camp

From free check-ups, consultations, and medicines, to heartfelt interactions — our dedicated team of doctors, volunteers, and supporters worked hand-in-hand to make a difference. Healthy individuals build a healthy society, and this camp was a step forward in that direction.

Clothes Drive

“Warmth isn’t just in clothes, it’s in kindness.” We are happy to share the successful completion of our Clothes Drive, an initiative to bring dignity and comfort to those in need. From warm clothes for winter to daily wear for children and adults, each donated item carried love, care, and hope.

Umbrella Drive for Street Vendors

“A little shade can bring a lot of relief.” Under the scorching sun or pouring rain, our street vendors work tirelessly to earn a living. Through our Umbrella Drive, we aimed to provide them with a small comfort — protection from harsh weather and a symbol of support. Each umbrella donated was not just a shield from sun and rain, but a gesture of respect for their hard work and resilience.

Birds Feeding Drive

“They can’t ask for food, but they deserve our care.” In a world where even the smallest creatures are struggling for survival, our Birds Feeding Initiative is a humble effort to give back to nature. With grains, water, and love — we’ve created safe feeding spots to ensure our feathered friends are not forgotten.

Dogs Feeding Drive

“They don’t have a voice, but they have a heart that feels.” Our Dogs Feeding Drive was a step towards showing love and care to our loyal street companions who often go unnoticed and unfed. With bowls of food, clean water, and a lot of affection, we reached out to feed the stray dogs in our community — not just to fill their stomachs, but to remind them they are not alone.

Summer Refreshment Drive

Because kindness tastes best when it's chilled. Under the scorching sun, a cool drink can be a lifesaver. Our Summer Refreshments Drive offered refreshing beverages to those in need — from street vendors to workers — bringing them comfort and care. It’s not just about quenching thirst, it’s about sharing kindness.

Monkey Feeding Drive

“Even the smallest act of kindness can make a difference.” Through our Monkey Feeding Drive, we extended our care to the innocent monkeys in our community. Offering them food, we not only helped feed them but also created awareness about the importance of caring for all creatures. A small gesture for a greater cause — protecting and respecting wildlife.

Fish Feeding Drive

“Every living being deserves care, no matter how small.” Through our Fish Feeding Drive, we reached out to the aquatic life in our community, providing them with food and showing that we are responsible stewards of nature. It’s not just about feeding fish — it’s about fostering respect for all life forms.

Cow Feeding Drive

“Compassion is the greatest form of kindness.” Through our Cow Feeding Drive, we offered food and care to the cows that roam our streets, providing them with nourishment and showing our respect for these gentle creatures. A small act of kindness towards animals can create a big change.

Book Donation Drive

“A book is a gift you can open again and again.” Through our Book Donation Drive, we’ve collected and distributed books to those who need them most—students, libraries, and individuals striving to learn and grow. A single book can spark a lifetime of learning, and your donation can change a life.

About Us

Our dedicated Aarjav Foundation team works selflessly to bring change, ensuring every initiative reaches those in need.
